You are given an informal bug report or note:
“Logout button doesn’t work on Safari. It just doesn’t respond.”

Use your natural language understanding to interpret the intent and meaning, and rewrite it as a well-structured bug ticket using the following format:

Title: A concise summary of the bug.
Description: A brief explanation of the issue.
Steps to Reproduce: A clear, step-by-step list showing how to trigger the bug.
Expected vs Actual Behavior: What should happen vs what actually happens.
Environment (if known): Browser, OS, device, app version, etc.
Severity or Impact: How much this affects users or the business.
Instructions:
Take any informal bug note and convert it into a ticket using the format above. If any information is missing, leave that section blank or note “Not specified.” Be clear and specific in your output.
